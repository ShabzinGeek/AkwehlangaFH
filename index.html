About Us

Akwehlanga Funeral Home, providing services that make a last impression on your loved ones. For a dignified funeral service for your family members. We are dedicated to giving you the finest service with prominence on Professionalism, Quality Services, Dignity and Comfort.

We provide great services

Akwehlanga Funeral Home was founded by Ms. Nelisiwe Magwaza who had envisioned helping mostly African community members and of low income to provide a dignified service for their beloved. With this vision she also kept in mind the opportunity these services can give back to the communities by providing job opportunities for the unemployed youth. The funeral home is an important part of any community.

At Akwehlanga Funeral Home we believe in tradition within our services and constantly remain up to par with our services. We consider it an honour to have a reputation of being the best in the business and continue to please families, rising above their expectations.

When it comes time to make the decision regarding the funeral home you will trust to care for a loved one or to take care of your pre-planning arrangements, we are here to assist you with the information about our funeral home and our valued services.

Our Services

We provide you with services to suit everybody. Funeral Covers and Plans are available upon request.

Funeral Cover

The death of a loved one is an event that all of us is likely to experience during our lifetimes, often on numerous occasions.The only adequate precautionary measure that can be taken to avoid financial strain when it does happen is to have a funeral plan.

Funeral Services

Funeral is the act to bury a deceased person in a cemetery or place of internment. At Akwehlanga Funeral Home strive we to deliver the most respectful, professional and dignified service to people of any race, sexual orientation, religion, or culture.

Remembrance Ceremony

Akwehlanga Funeral Home also provide services that include paper and electronic products to celebrate the life of the deceased. Digital photographs and films will be used during the reception and posted on our website, if desired.

Hybrid/Virtual Funeral Service

Due to Covid-19 Rules & Regulations and its' limitations, Akwehlanga Funeral Home also provides a dignified virtual funeral to be attended by your long distance families from the comfort of their homes.

Refrigeration

Refrigeration is required for viewing of the body beyond 72 hours after death if there is no embalming. Embalming the body of the deceased is available when required.

Cremation

Akwehlanga Funeral Home arranges Cremation Services for families who prefer cremation to a burial service. We also arrange for those families who want to see the Cremation to burial service process. We also assist families who want to scatter ashes. Urns are also available
